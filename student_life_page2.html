    Introduction

    All Rivermont students in 5th-12th grade are encouraged to participate in school-sponsored athletics, regardless of ability level. Emphasis is placed on sportsmanship, teamwork, leadership, and confidence. Lessons learned on the field or court develop the character that is an essential component of the Rivermont Experience.

    Rivermont-sponsored athletics include volleyball, basketball, track, and cross country. The Rivermont Lions compete against schools in the area, including Morning Star Academy, Faith Christian School, North Scott High School, Eagle Ridge School, Camanche High School, and numerous others. Students in 9th-12th grade who wish to participate in sports not offered by Rivermont Collegiate may try out for the Bettendorf High School team of their choosing. Contact Michelle Grimm for more information.

    Clinton High has a baseball, boys/girls basketball, bowling, cross country, football, boys/girls golf, boys/girls soccer, softball, boys/girls swimming, boys/girls tennis, boys/girls track, volleyball, wrestling, and cheer team. It is also part of the Mississippi Athletic Conference with ten other schools: Bettendorf High School, Central DeWitt High School, Clinton High School, Davenport Assumption High School, Davenport Central High School, Davenport North High School, Davenport West High School, Muscatine High School, North Scott High School, and Pleasant Valley High School.
