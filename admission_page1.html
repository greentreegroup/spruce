Our Admission Process

1. Speak to Admissons

Our Admissions Consultants will invite you to an in-person or online consultation – so you can get to know us and we can get to know you.

2. Gather admissions documents

Documents include:

  • Transcripts or report cards from last two years
  • Scan of your passport
  • Student photo
  • Documentation regarding medical conditions, learning accommodations, and mental health, if applicable

3. Complete an online application

Create your student profile and upload the requested admissions documents.

4. Attend interview

This is your chance to tell us about your interests and ambitions, academic commitment, and motivation for coming to Spruce Education. It is our chance to get to know you "off paper" for the best feel of how you will fit in and stand out as member of our school community.

5. Admission decision

After making a holistic assessment of your application, you will receive our admissions decision. Successful applicants will receive an acceptance pack and our Admissions Consultants will help you finalize any documentation closer to departure. If you are traveling abroad to the UK, we will help obtain your visa.

6. Prepare for new adventure!

Get ready to start your journey!
