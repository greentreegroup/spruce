    About Spruce Education

    Spruce Education is a premier education consulting company that specializes in helping high school students in China gain admission to top US universities. We have years of experience working with Chinese education consultants and high school headmasters to identify the best students and connect them with top US high schools.

    Spruce opens a world of opportunities for high school students by providing them with a superior education, thorough preparation for university and a future that knows no borders.

    Who We Are

    We are an international team truly passionate about education. We are a team of experienced educators and consultants who are passionate about helping students achieve their full potential. Our team includes experts in education, admissions, and international relations, who are dedicated to helping Chinese students succeed in the US.

    What We Do

    At Spruce Education, we offer a comprehensive range of services designed to help Chinese students navigate the complex process of applying to US high schools and universities. Our services include student recruitment, application assistance, interview preparation, and cultural training. Unlock your potential at a boarding school that unites quality academics, co-curricular learning opportunities and personalized university guidance in a truly international campus environment.

    What We Offer

    We offer a unique opportunity for Chinese high school students to attend top US high schools, including Rivermont Academy High School and Clinton High. By enrolling in a US high school, Chinese students can gain valuable cultural experience and become eligible for admission to top US universities.

    In addition, we personalize counseling and support to help students succeed academically and socially while studying in the US. One-to-one meetings with a designated university advisor, application workshops, test preparation and interview coaching are part of what set Clinton High Schools graduates apart when applying to university.

    Whether you're a high school student in China or an education consultant looking to partner with us, Spruce Education is here to help you achieve your goals. Contact us today to learn more about our services and how we can help you succeed.
