    Steps to Enrollment

    Our admission process is designed with your ease in mind—just follow the steps below and you'll be on your way to becoming a student. We encourage you to apply by August 1 for the fall semester and December 1 for the spring semester to ensure you have enough time to complete the steps to enrollment before the start of the semester. Each type of student listed below requires a slightly different application process. Click on each link and read the descriptions carefully, then follow the steps to admission for the description that fits you best:
